Rock 77

This is an art project that blends my history of listening to rock music in the past 6 years. The 77 albums that I like and listened to most often are displayed, forming the Chinese characters "Rock". It can also be explored in a stack view, where the albums are sorted by their release dates. The timeline will guide you through in both views.

Each album will be accompanied with photos and text depicting the state I was in when I was listening to it. The music, coming from different time periods, blends in my life history, becoming anchors, mirrors, even part of me.

Made by Ningxia Zhang in 2014.
